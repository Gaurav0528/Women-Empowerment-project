GREAT WOMEN

MARIE CURIE

Marie Curie is remembered for her discovery of radium and polonium, and her huge contribution to finding treatments for cancer.

GUNJAN SAXENA

Flight lieutenant Gunjan Saxena was the first Indian Air Force woman officer who went to war. She made history during the Kargil war in 1999 and rescued several soldiers.

SUCHETA KRIPALANI

Sucheta Kripalani was an Indian freedom fighter and politician. She was India's first woman Chief Minister, serving as the head of the Uttar Pradesh government.

MARY KOM

Mary Kom is the only female to become World Amateur Boxing champion for a record six times, and the only boxer (male or female) to win eight World Championship medals.
